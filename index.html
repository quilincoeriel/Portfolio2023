About me

Let’s start with a little background information about me. As you already know my name is Qui-Lin Coeriel. I was born on the sunny island of Curaçao. My native language is Papiamentu, but I can also speak Dutch, English, and Spanish. Before coming to the Netherlands, I studied Administration at Frater Aurelio SBO.

After finishing my administration degree, I decided that I wanted to study something that I truly have a passion for. During my internship at Media School Curaçao, I decided that I am going to study Communication & Multimedia Design at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. Five years later here we are. Successfully completed the study.

I would describe myself as a Poké Bowl. Why, may you ask? The Poké Bowl consist of different ingredients. Like me, I have different skills and characteristics that make me an equipped UX Designer. I am always looking forward to developing myself and growing in every way possible. As a UX designer, I find that there is always a solution to a problem.

Fun Fact about me: My favourite cartoon character is Olaf. The reason why I love this character is that I can see myself in him. He always has a positive attitude no matter the situation and when there is a problem always looks for a solution.
